A Catalyst for Growth

WorldSkills India makes skills aspirational, inspires young talent, drives sectoral development, and helps stimulate the country’s economy.

  • Empower

    We empower the youth of India, through state and national-level competitions, and guide them to realise their full potential.

  • Engage

    We engage with governments, industry and trainers to provide valuable skilling resources and strengthen our global representation.

  • Excellence

    We strive for excellence by sharpening skills as per global standards and preparing candidates for international platforms.
